Welcome to Lesotho

Lesotho is a beautiful mountain kingdom located inside South Africa. It is famous for its breathtaking landscapes, traditional culture, and adventure tourism such as pony trekking and mountain hiking.

Tourists visit Lesotho to explore the highlands, experience Basotho culture, and enjoy nature in places like the Maloti Mountains.
